# TIKTOK CONTENT STRATEGY: Vitality Score Series
**Format:** Short-form vertical video (15-60 seconds)  
**Tone:** Educational, honest, behind-the-scenes property improvement  
**Platform Strategy:** Use Vitality Analysis as content backbone for 8-video series + evergreen content

---

## SERIES OVERVIEW: "Why My Airbnbs Have Different Ratings"

### Hook Concept:
*"I analyzed 8 of my Airbnbs with a special framework. Here's what I found (and it shocked me)."*

This frames the Vitality Score analysis as legitimate, transparent, and insider knowledge. Viewers get actionable insights while you build authority in the space.

---

## VIDEO CONTENT CALENDAR (8-Part Series)

### VIDEO 1: THE INTRO HOOK (30 seconds)
**Title:** "I Scored 8 of My Airbnbs (Results Are Messy)"

**Content:**
- Quick montage of all 8 properties (3-4 sec each)
- Reveal Vitality Scores on screen as you walk through (emphasize range 51-93)
- "Why do these have such different ratings?"
- "The answer isn't what I expected..."

**Metrics:**
- Target Hook: "I did a design audit on my portfolio"
- CTA: "Here's what I learned (Thread 👇)"

**Production Notes:**
- Use b-roll from each property
- Add text overlays with Vitality scores
- Upbeat, pattern-interrupting music

---

### VIDEO 2: HOBBY LOBBY (THE GOLD STANDARD) (45 seconds)
**Title:** "Why This $176 Airbnb Gets Perfect Reviews (5.0⭐)"

**Content:**
- Tour key spaces (10 sec): Living room lighting, kitchen emerald cabinets, bedroom
- Reveal Vitality Score: 93/100 (A-)
- Breakdown 3 reasons it wins:
  1. Layered lighting strategy (show before/after concept)
  2. Coordinated colour story (emerald + gold + charcoal)
  3. Professional staging details (throw pillows, plant styling)
- CTA: "This is the standard I'm chasing with my other properties"

**Metrics:**
- Show rating badge (5.0⭐)
- Display score progression: 93/100
- Engagement hook: "Could you design a space this good?"

---

### VIDEO 3: BROWN TOWN (THE RESCUE) (60 seconds)
**Title:** "$173 Airbnb Gets DEMOLISHED (Then Fixed)"

**Content:**
- Show problem: Brown vintage furniture in modern shell
- Overlay score: 51/100 (D-) with emphasis on "yikes"
- Show issue breakdown:
  - "Colour chaos" (5 different wood tones)
  - "Style identity crisis" (what IS this aesthetic?)
  - "Cramped secondary bedroom"
- Reveal solution: Replace furniture with modern sectional
- "One piece changed EVERYTHING"
- Before/after comparison (stacked frames side-by-side)
- New projected score: 79/100 (+28 points!)
- Revenue impact: "+$872/month"

**Metrics:**
- Dramatic score jump visualization (51→79)
- Dollar amount ($872) prominently displayed
- ROI: "1,200 investment, earned back in 2 weeks"

**Production Notes:**
- Use shocking/critical music for "before" section
- Switch to positive music at "here's the fix"
- Add text: "Single piece of furniture changed everything"

---

### VIDEO 4: FREE WILL (THE PARADOX) (45 seconds)
**Title:** "4.91⭐ Rating at $60/Night (But Score is LOW)"

**Content:**
- Show score: 53/100 (D)
- BUT rating: 4.91⭐ (nearly perfect)
- "How is this possible?"
- Explanation: Honest positioning > visual polish
- "I'm NOT overproducing photos"
- "Guests expect budget, get clean, leave delighted"
- Vs. other properties: "I promise luxury, deliver medium, guests disappointed"
- Insight: "Expectations > Reality"

**Metrics:**
- Juxtapose score (53) vs. rating (4.91)
- Show guest quote (from reviews) about value
- Lesson: "What if your marketing was more honest?"

**Production Notes:**
- Show "messy" kitchen photos (unstaged)
- Contrast with overstaged competitor photos
- Tone: Slightly self-aware, humble

---

### VIDEO 5: GRAYDAR (THE REFINED) (45 seconds)
**Title:** "Sophisticated Design That Photographers Love (4.88⭐)"

**Content:**
- Tour grey-scale aesthetic (modern luxury vibes)
- Score: 72/100 (C) — "Good, not great"
- Why it scores well: Committed design vision
- "But there's room to grow..."
- Show 3 easy upgrades:
  1. Add warm brass lamps (show image)
  2. Consistent bedroom photos
  3. Strategic plants for balance
- Projected new score: 82/100 (+10)
- Rate increase: $125 → $145 (+$20/night)

**Metrics:**
- Before/after lighting comparison (warm vs. clinical)
- ROI: "+$768/month for $550 investment"
- Timeline: "1 month to implement"

---

### VIDEO 6: DELTA BLUE (THE LIGHTHOUSE) (45 seconds)
**Title:** "Fixing a 4.66⭐ Rating (One Problem at a Time)"

**Content:**
- Score: 55/100 (D+)
- Rating: 4.66⭐ (below portfolio average)
- Key issues shown visually:
  1. Cave-like lighting (show before footage)
  2. Unmade beds (staging failure)
  3. Refrigerator in living room (????)
- Top ROI fix: Bedroom staging overhaul
  - "New bedding, pillows, nightstands"
  - Cost: $150-250
  - Impact: +15-20 points
- Show completed result
- New projected: 77/100
- Rate increase justified: $167 → $180

**Metrics:**
- "Fridge in living room" gets laugh reaction
- Show simple fixes (throw pillows, bedding)
- Emphasize: "Not expensive, huge impact"

---

### VIDEO 7: LOWER LUXURY (FAMILY PLAY) (60 seconds)
**Title:** "Why This 6-Guest Property Feels Like a Basement (And How I Fixed It)"

**Content:**
- Score: 61/100 (D+)
- Problem: Institutional lighting in basement space
- "Harsh overhead lights = goodbye luxury vibes"
- Show before photos (clinical, cold)
- Solution: Warm lighting transformation
- "Brass lamps, dimmer switches, warm bulbs"
- Show after (dramatic difference)
- Why this matters: "Families expect warmth"
- New score: 83/100
- Rate increase: $186 → $210 (JUSTIFIED)

**Metrics:**
- Before/after side-by-side lighting comparison
- Show family use case (cooking together benefit)
- Emphasize: "Lighting is the ROI hack"

---

### VIDEO 8: RENT FREE (THE HARD TRUTH) (45 seconds)
**Title:** "I Overpriced This Airbnb (And Here's How I Fixed It)"

**Content:**
- Score: 53/100 (D)
- Rate: $200 (highest in portfolio)
- Rating: 4.6⭐ (lowest in portfolio)
- "Guests felt ripped off"
- Two options shown visually:
  - Option A: Invest $5K to justify $200
  - Option B: Drop to $125, stop disappointing guests
- "I chose honesty"
- New rate: $125 (matches quality)
- Projected rating: 4.80⭐
- Lesson: "Price-quality mismatch damages everything"

**Metrics:**
- Show budget ($200 vs. $125)
- Emphasize rating recovery (4.6 → 4.8)
- Life lesson tone: "Sometimes you're wrong, fix it"

**Production Notes:**
- Vulnerable, honest tone
- Show this helps overall brand
- Lesson for viewers: "Audit your own pricing"

## MEASUREMENT & ITERATION

### Metrics to Track:
- **View Count:** Target 10K+ views per property video
- **Engagement Rate:** Target 3-5% (likes/comments/shares)
- **Click-Through:** Track link clicks to Vitality Reports
- **Follower Growth:** Expect 50-100 new followers per week during series
- **Website Traffic:** Monitor visits from TikTok to property listings

### Success Indicators:
- Video 1 hooks 10K+ viewers
- Property videos generate booking inquiries
- Comments reveal audience pain points (design/pricing/staging)
- Share rate indicates resonance (target: >2% of views)

### Iteration Points:
- If score-focused videos underperform, shift to before/afters
- If educational content overperforms, create "Design Audit 101" series
- If specific properties get high engagement, create dedicated playlists
- Monitor TikTok analytics weekly; adjust tone/style as needed
