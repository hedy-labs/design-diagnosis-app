## SPECIAL ANALYSIS: Why Honest Marketing Works

Free Will is a textbook example of **expectations management** outperforming **visual seduction**:

- Guest books $60 property
- Guest sees unstaged kitchen (sets low bar visually)
- Guest sees minimal decor (sets low bar aesthetically)
- Guest arrives to: clean space, working appliances, friendly host
- **Guest compares reality to (lowered) expectations: Exceeds = 5 stars**

Versus:
- Guest books $90 "luxury" property with overproduced photos
- Guest sees pristine styled kitchen (sets high bar visually)
- Guest sees designer decor (sets high bar aesthetically)
- Guest arrives to: clean space, working appliances, friendly host
- **Guest compares reality to (raised) expectations: Meets = 4 stars**

**This is why honesty is a competitive advantage at budget price points.**
