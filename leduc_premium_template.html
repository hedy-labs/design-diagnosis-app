Pillar 1: Spatial Flow & Ergonomics

The 60cm Rule, Landing Strips, and Floating Furniture
Score: --/25
The 60cm Walkway Rule

Every walkway between furniture must be at least 60cm wide. Below this threshold, guests feel cramped and must navigate sideways.

⚠️ Floating Furniture Detection

Your sofa is positioned in the middle of the room (not against a wall). This is "floating furniture."

CRITICAL RULE: Never recommend gallery walls or wall-mounted art above floating furniture. Instead:

  • ✅ Ground the sofa with an 8x10 area rug
  • ✅ Add a console table behind it as a subtle divider
  • ✅ Use floating shelves (wall-mounted but minimal visual weight)
Landing Strip (Entry Access)

Guests need minimum 1 meter of clear floor space at entry for luggage drop. Your entryway is compact.

Overhead-Only Penalty, Color Temperature, and Institutional Feel
Score: --/20
⚠️ The Overhead-Only Penalty

Your bedroom relies on a single overhead light plus a small bedside lamp. This creates a clinical, institutional feel that says "hotel hallway" not "relaxing escape."

Cost impact: -3 points from pillar score.

Color Temperature Assessment

Layering Solution

Upgrade to task + accent + ambient lighting:
