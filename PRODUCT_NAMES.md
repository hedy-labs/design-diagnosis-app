## Primary Product: "Design Diagnosis"

**Target:** Airbnb/VRBO short-term rental hosts  
**Metric:** Vitality Score (0–100)  
**Tagline:** "Professional design audit in minutes"

### Why Design Diagnosis
- Positions you as expert (not generic algorithm)
- Medical authority ("diagnosis" = professional, thorough)
- Premium anchor (justifies $29–$49 pricing)
- Defensible brand (hard for competitors to claim diagnosis without expertise)
- TikTok friendly ("I got a design diagnosis and it changed my listing")

### How It Works
1. Upload listing photos OR paste Airbnb link
2. AI analyzes design across 5 dimensions (Colour, Lighting, Anchors, Clutter, Staging)
3. Get Vitality Score (0–100) + room-by-room diagnostic
4. View Top 3 Priority Fixes (with cost estimates)
5. Shop with Affiliate Links (Value/Signature/Luxury tiers)
6. (Optional) Buy Premium Report PDF or Book Consultation

### Pricing
- Free Vitality Report (with affiliate links)
- Premium Report: $25–$29 (PDF, takes-home friendly)
- Consultation: $99 (15 min with Rachel)

## Secondary Product: "StageMate"

**Target:** Home sellers needing staging advice before listing  
**Metric:** Readiness Score (0–100)  
**Launch:** Phase 2, Week 13+ (after Design Diagnosis validates)

### Why StageMate
- Clear, memorable, complementary to Design Diagnosis
- "Mate" implies partnership (staging partner for your home sale)
- Different audience, same framework (scalable backend)

### How It Works
- Same workflow as Design Diagnosis, different weights
- Includes moving company/storage affiliate links (higher AOV)
- Optional virtual staging photos ($99–$199/room)
- Optional consultation ($199–$299/home)

### Positioning
- Design Diagnosis for rentals (increase monthly revenue)
- StageMate for selling (maximize sale price)
