## FIVE PILLARS FRAMEWORK

### Pillar 1: SPATIAL FLOW & ERGONOMICS (25 points)

**The 60cm Walkway Rule**
- Every walkway (between bed/wall, sofa/coffee table) must be ≥60cm
- Failure: "Collision Hazard" — guest must squeeze sideways
- Scoring:
  - All walkways ≥60cm: +6 points
  - 1-2 walkways <60cm: -2 points
  - 3+ walkways <60cm: -5 points
- Photo detection: Can guest walk straight without angling sideways?

**Landing Strip (Entry Clear Space)**
- Requirement: Minimum 1 meter of clear floor at entry for luggage
- Scoring:
  - Clear 1m+ radius at entry: +5 points
  - Entry cluttered with furniture/racks: -3 points
  - Completely blocked: -5 points
- Photo detection: Can you see floor immediately inside door?

**Bilateral Bed Access**
- Beds in units >$100/night: 50cm clear on BOTH sides (required)
- Scoring:
  - Both sides ≥50cm: +5 points
  - One side <50cm: -2 to -5 points (tier-dependent)
  - Both sides <50cm: -5 points
- Photo detection: Is floor visible on both sides of bed?

**Kitchen Triangle (Fridge → Sink → Stove)**
- Requirement: Unobstructed path between all three
- Scoring:
  - Triangle clear, direct paths: +5 points
  - One leg blocked: -2 points
  - Two+ legs blocked: -3 points
- Photo detection: Can guest move freely between appliances?

**⚠️ FLOATING FURNITURE DETECTION (Critical Rule)**
- Detect: Sofa positioned away from walls, beds in room center, floating islands
- Signal: Visible floor around multiple furniture sides
- **CRITICAL RULE:** Never recommend wall-based fixes (gallery walls, shelving, wall-mounted art) for floating furniture
- Instead recommend: Floor anchors, area rugs, floating shelves, console tables
- Example conflict:
  - ❌ WRONG: "Add gallery wall above floating sofa"
  - ✅ RIGHT: "Ground floating sofa with area rug + side table"

### Pillar 2: LIGHTING & OPTICAL HEALTH (20 points)

**Overhead-Only Penalty**
- Single overhead light = institutional ambiance = -3 points
- Scoring:
  - Task + accent + ambient layering: +6 points
  - Task + accent (no ambient): +4 points
  - Overhead + one task lamp: +2 points
  - Overhead only: 0 points

**Color Temperature Assessment**
- Warm (2700-3000K): +2 (relaxing)
- Neutral (4000-4100K): +1 (clinical)
- Cool (>5000K): -1 (institutional)
- Mixed temperatures in same room: -1 (jarring)

**Glare Analysis**
- Bare bulbs visible: -1
- Glare on screen/mirror: -1
- Window glare uncontrolled: -1
- All controlled: +1

## SOVEREIGNTY VS SURVEILLANCE ASSESSMENT

### SOVEREIGNTY (WELCOMED — Guest Feels Free)

**The Path of Least Resistance:**
- Clear floor space visible from entry: +1
- Available/empty hooks: +1
- Clear surfaces: +1
- Warm light visible from entry (beacon effect): +1

**The Escape Route (Critical for Bedrooms):**
- 50cm+ buffer on both sides of bed: +3
- Psychological freedom = safety = rest = good reviews

**Permission Signals:**
- "You can use this" vibe
- Storage transparent (guest can see where things are)
- Open, inviting layout

### SURVEILLANCE (TRAPPED — Guest Feels Watched)

**Host-Heavy Decor:**
- Rule signs ("Do Not Touch," "Do Not Sit"): -2 per sign
- Fragile items on low tables: -1 per item
- Busy patterns dominating: -3
- Family photos in guest spaces: -1 per photo

**Total Surveillance Score >5:** Flag as "Host-Heavy Risk" = -10 points from vitality
