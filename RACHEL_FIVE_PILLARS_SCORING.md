## PILLAR 1: SPATIAL FLOW & ERGONOMICS (25 Points)

### The 60cm Rule (Walkway Minimum)
**Requirement:** Every walkway (between bed/wall, sofa/coffee table) must be at least 60cm.

**Failure:** "Collision Hazard" = guest must squeeze sideways, can't walk freely

**Scoring:**
- All walkways ≥60cm: +6 points
- 1–2 walkways <60cm: −2 points
- 3+ walkways <60cm or single tight corridor: −5 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Can guest walk straight through without angling sideways?
- Is there visible floor between furniture pieces?
- Do doorways appear to have ≥60cm clearance beyond door swing?

---

### The Landing Strip (Entry Clear Space)
**Requirement:** Minimum 1 meter of clear floor space at entry for luggage drop

**Failure:** Guest arrives with luggage, nowhere to drop bags = immediate friction

**Scoring:**
- Clear 1m+ radius at entry: +5 points
- Entry cluttered with coat rack, shoes, furniture: −3 points
- Completely blocked entry: −5 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Can you see floor space immediately inside door?
- Is entry furniture-free or minimal (hooks on walls, not racks)?
- Is doorway accessible without moving items?

---

### Bilateral Access (Bed Buffer)
**Requirement:** Beds in units >$100/night must have 50cm of space on BOTH sides

**Failure:** One person trapped in bed, must climb over partner = claustrophobia

**Scoring:**
- Both sides ≥50cm clear: +5 points
- One side <50cm: −2 points (mid-tier: −3 premium: −5)
- Both sides <50cm (wall-hugger): −5 points
- Primary bedroom in luxury unit: critical (−7 if missing)

**Photo Detection:**
- Can you see floor on both sides of bed in photos?
- Is bed centered in room or pushed to one wall?
- Is there visible distance between bed + nightstands on both sides?

---

### The Triangle of Use (Kitchen Efficiency)
**Requirement:** Path between Fridge → Sink → Stove must be unobstructed

**Failure:** Guest trying to cook must navigate obstacle course = meal prep frustration

**Scoring:**
- Triangle clear, direct paths: +5 points
- One leg of triangle blocked (e.g., island in middle): −2 points
- Two+ legs blocked or L-shaped kitchen: −3 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Is there visible counter/floor space between each appliance?
- Can you trace an unobstructed path?
- Are islands/furniture positioned to block flow?

---

### Seating Capacity Match
**Requirement:** If unit sleeps 4, comfortable lounge seating for 4 (not 2 sofa + 2 dining chairs)

**Failure:** 2 people feel excluded from main living space = review mentions "not enough seating"

**Scoring:**
- Lounge seating matches guest count (e.g., 4-seat sectional for 4 guests): +4 points
- Seating ≥ guest count but divided (2 on sofa, 2 on armchairs): +2 points
- Seating < guest count (2-seat sofa for 4 guests): −3 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Count visible seating surfaces in main living area
- Is main seating a sofa/sectional or just dining chairs?
- Does seating capacity match guest count?

## PILLAR 2: LIGHTING & OPTICAL HEALTH (20 Points)

### The Rule of Three (Light Sources)
**Requirement:** Every room needs THREE light sources:
1. **Overhead** (ceiling fixture)
2. **Task** (desk lamp, reading lamp, bedside lamp)
3. **Ambient/Mood** (wall sconces, floor lamp, accent lighting)

**Failure:** Room with ONLY overhead light = harsh, institutional, prevents melatonin

**Scoring:**
- All three light types visible: +7 points
- Two light types: +4 points
- Only overhead light: 0 points (Visual Interrogation flag)
- No lights visible: −3 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Count distinct light sources in each room photo
- Overhead (ceiling fixture)?
- Task lighting (bedside, desk, reading)?
- Ambient (wall sconce, floor lamp, accent)?

---

### Kelvin Consistency (Color Temperature)
**Requirement:** All bulbs must be 2700K–3000K (Warm White). NO mixing with 5000K daylight.

**Failure:** Mixing warm + daylight = "Optical Fatigue" (guest's eyes feel tired)

**Scoring:**
- All bulbs warm (2700–3000K): +7 points
- Mixed (some 2700, some 5000): −4 points (Optical Fatigue flag)
- All daylight/cool white (5000K+): −3 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Do light bulbs appear warm yellow/orange or cool white?
- Is lighting consistent across room?
- Are there multiple light sources with different warmth?

---

### The Mirror Test (Bathroom Lighting)
**Requirement:** Bathroom mirrors must have side-lighting OR top-lighting that illuminates face (not shadow-eyes)

**Failure:** Guest looks in mirror, eyes in shadow = feels tired/old, affects mood/review

**Scoring:**
- Side-lit mirror (vanity lights on both sides): +3 points
- Top-lit mirror (sufficient light): +2 points
- Ceiling-only light (shadow-eyes): −2 points
- No visible mirror lighting: −3 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Is mirror visible in bathroom photos?
- Are there lights next to or above mirror?
- Would guest's face be illuminated (not shadowed)?

---

### The Dark-Out Standard (Bedroom Blackout)
**Requirement:** Bedrooms must have blockout window treatments. If light leaks at edges = "Circadian Fail"

**Failure:** Light leaking at 5 AM wakes guest = poor sleep = lower review

**Scoring:**
- Full blackout curtains/shades, no light leak: +3 points
- Partial blackout, minimal light leak at edges: +1 point
- Thin curtains, visible light leak: −2 points (Circadian Fail)
- No window treatment: −3 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Are bedroom windows covered?
- Do curtains appear thick/opaque or sheer/thin?
- Are there visible light gaps at edges?

## PILLAR 3: SENSORY & TEXTILE LOGIC (20 Points)

### Natural Fiber Bias (Linens & Towels)
**Requirement:** Sheets and towels must be Cotton or Linen. Polyester/Microfiber = "Synthetic Friction" penalty

**Failure:** Polyester sheets don't breathe → guest wakes up sweating → automatic 3-star

**Scoring:**
- Cotton/linen sheets AND cotton/linen towels: +7 points
- One natural (sheets or towels), one synthetic: +3 points
- All synthetic sheets/towels: 0 points (Synthetic Friction flag)
- Worn/pilled natural fibers: +4 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Do sheets appear soft/natural (matte finish) or shiny/slippery (polyester)?
- Do towels appear fluffy/natural or thin/synthetic?
- Is there visible pilling (quality indicator)?

---

### The Triple Sheet Protocol (Hygiene Transparency)
**Requirement:** Top sheet between guest and duvet/comforter (North American standard for hygiene)

**Failure:** Duvet-only (European) perceived as unsanitary = guest anxiety about duvet washing

**Scoring:**
- Top sheet + duvet visible: +4 points
- Duvet-only (no visible top sheet): −3 points (Hygiene Anxiety flag)
- Both present but visible wear/stains: +2 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Are two distinct sheet layers visible (top sheet + duvet)?
- Or is there only a comforter/duvet visible?

---

### Rug Scaling (Furniture Anchoring)
**Requirement:** Front legs of sofa/chairs must sit ON the rug. Floating rug = "unanchored" feeling

**Failure:** Rug in center of room, furniture around edges = space feels disconnected

**Scoring:**
- Furniture front legs on rug: +4 points
- Partial rug (some front legs on, some off): +1 point
- Floating rug or no rug: −2 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Is rug visible under furniture or separate?
- Do sofa/chair legs sit on rug or float above?
- Does rug anchor furniture cluster?

---

### Acoustic Softening (Echo Chamber Prevention)
**Requirement:** At least 30% of "hard" surfaces (tile/wood) must be covered by textiles (rugs, curtains, pillows)

**Failure:** All hard surfaces = "Echo Chamber" effect = guest voice echoes, feels institutional

**Scoring:**
- ≥30% hard surfaces covered by textiles: +5 points
- 15–29% covered: +2 points
- <15% covered (mostly bare tile/hardwood): −3 points (Echo Chamber flag)

**Photo Detection:**
- What % of visible flooring is rug vs. bare?
- Are there curtains absorbing sound?
- Are there pillows/throws on furniture?
- Calculate: (rug + curtain + textile coverage) / (total surfaces)

## PILLAR 4: POWER & CONNECTIVITY (15 Points)

### The 1-Meter Outlet Rule
**Requirement:** Functional power outlet within 1 meter of:
- Every bedside (for phone/tablet charging)
- Primary work surface (desk/dining table)

**Failure:** Guest must choose between phone charging or lamp = Power Desert friction

**Scoring:**
- Outlets within 1m of all bedside + work areas: +6 points
- Outlets within 1.5m or some missing: +2 points
- Outlets >1.5m away or missing bedside: −2 points
- Visible extension cords or outlets blocked: −3 points (Power Desert)

**Photo Detection:**
- Are outlets visible in bedside area?
- Are outlets visible near desk/work surface?
- Are there visible extension cords (red flag for shortage)?

---

### The USB-C Standard (Modern Charging)
**Requirement:** Modern listings should have integrated charging ports in nightstands or lamps (USB-C ideal)

**Failure:** No integrated charging = guest digs for adapter or uses floor outlet = friction

**Scoring:**
- USB-C ports in nightstands or lamps: +5 points
- USB-A ports visible: +3 points
- Traditional outlets only: +1 point
- No visible charging infrastructure: 0 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Are there USB ports visible in nightstand?
- Are lamp bases designed for charging?
- Are there power strips with USB?

---

### Visible Speed Test (WiFi Credibility)
**Requirement:** Screenshot of Speedtest (>50Mbps) in photo gallery (for 100-point score consideration)

**Failure:** No speed proof = guest arrives expecting fast WiFi, finds slow = immediate 1-star complaint

**Scoring:**
- Speedtest screenshot visible (>50Mbps): +4 points
- WiFi visible but no speed proof: +2 points
- No WiFi visible or slow speed proof: 0 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Is there a Speedtest image in listing photos?
- Does it show >50Mbps (modern guest expectation)?

## PILLAR 5: INTENTIONALITY & "THE SOUL" (20 Points)

### The Found Item Ban (Furniture Coherence)
**Requirement:** No mismatched "leftover" furniture. Every piece must share color, material, or era-DNA.

**Failure:** Random dresser + random bed + random chairs = "assembled, not curated" = −5 Staging Integrity

**Scoring:**
- All furniture shares coherent aesthetic (material, era, or color): +7 points
- Mostly coherent with 1–2 pieces off: +4 points
- Mismatched "basement leftovers": 0 points (Chaotic flag)

**Photo Detection:**
- Do furniture pieces appear to belong together?
- Are materials consistent (all wood, all metal, all mixed)?
- Is there a visible design story?

---

### Art Scaling (Proportional Balance)
**Requirement:** Wall art must be at least 60% of width of furniture it hangs over

**Failure:** Tiny art on big wall = "lonely and amateur" = looks cheap

**Scoring:**
- Art 60%+ width of furniture: +4 points
- Art 40–60% width: +2 points
- Art <40% width or missing: −2 points

**Photo Detection:**
- Is there art visible on walls?
- Does art width compare to furniture below it?
- Is art appropriately scaled?

---

### Functional Decor (No Dust Collectors)
**Requirement:** Decor should be "active" (books, local guides, plants) NOT "passive" (plastic fruit, non-functional knick-knacks)

**Failure:** Plastic decorations signal "budget" and "lifeless" = −1 Staging Integrity per dust collector

**Scoring:**
- Active decor only (books, real plants, guides): +5 points
- Mostly active with minimal static decor: +3 points
- Multiple dust collectors visible: −2 points per item

**Photo Detection:**
- Are decorative items functional (books, plants, guides)?
- Are there plastic/non-functional items visible?

---

### The Host Ghost Filter (Personal Item Removal)
**Requirement:** Removal of personal photos, religious icons, political messaging = guest feels "Sovereign"

**Failure:** Personal items = guest feels watched = "Surveillance" mode = lower review

**Scoring:**
- Zero personal items visible: +4 points
- 1–2 personal items visible: 0 points
- 3+ personal items or religious icons: −3 points (Host's Ghost flag)

**Photo Detection:**
- Family photos visible anywhere?
- Religious items visible?
- Personal memorabilia?
- Motivational quotes with host name?

        spatial_score += 4

        spatial_score += 2
    else:
        spatial_score -= 3

        lighting_score += 7

        lighting_score += 4

        lighting_score += 0  # flag: Visual Interrogation

    if all_bulbs_2700_3000K:
        lighting_score += 7
    elif mixed_temps:
        lighting_score -= 4  # flag: Optical Fatigue
    elif all_cool_white:
        lighting_score -= 3
    
    # Mirror Test
    if bathroom_mirror_side_or_top_lit:
        lighting_score += 3
    elif bathroom_mirror_ceiling_only:
        lighting_score -= 2
    
    # Dark-Out Standard

        lighting_score += 3
    elif bedroom_has_partial_blackout:
        lighting_score += 1
    elif light_leaks_visible:
        lighting_score -= 2  # flag: Circadian Fail
    
    # PILLAR 3: Sensory & Textile Logic (20 points max)
    textile_score = 0
    
    # Natural Fiber Bias
    if sheets_cotton_and_towels_cotton:
        textile_score += 7
    elif one_natural_one_synthetic:
        textile_score += 3
    elif all_synthetic:
        textile_score += 0  # flag: Synthetic Friction
    
    # Triple Sheet Protocol
    if top_sheet_and_duvet_visible:
        textile_score += 4
    elif duvet_only_no_top_sheet:
        textile_score -= 3  # flag: Hygiene Anxiety
    
    # Rug Scaling
    if furniture_front_legs_on_rug:
        textile_score += 4
    elif partial_rug:
        textile_score += 1
    elif floating_rug:
        textile_score -= 2
    
    # Acoustic Softening
    textile_coverage = calculate_hard_surface_coverage(property_photos)
    if textile_coverage >= 0.30:
        textile_score += 5
    elif textile_coverage >= 0.15:
        textile_score += 2
    else:
        textile_score -= 3  # flag: Echo Chamber
    
    # PILLAR 4: Power & Connectivity (15 points max)
    power_score = 0
    
    # 1-Meter Outlet Rule
    if outlets_within_1m_bedside_and_desk:
        power_score += 6
    elif outlets_within_1.5m:

    # USB-C Standard

        power_score += 5
    elif usb_a_ports_visible:
        power_score += 3
    else:
        power_score += 1
    
    # WiFi Speed Test
    if speedtest_screenshot_visible_and_50mbps_plus:
        power_score += 4
    elif wifi_visible_but_no_proof:
        power_score += 2
    else:
        power_score += 0
    
    # PILLAR 5: Intentionality & Soul (20 points max)
    soul_score = 0

        soul_score += 7
    elif mostly_coherent:

    if active_decor > dust_collectors:
        soul_score += 5

    elif personal_items <= 2:
        soul_score += 0

        soul_score -= 3  # flag: Host's Ghost
    
    # TOTAL

    return {
