# Design Diagnosis Report: "Color Blind"
**Edmonton Basement Suite | 2BR/1BA | $102 CAD/night | 4.92⭐ Rating**

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Overall Design Score: 62/100** | **Tier: C+ (Underperforming vs. Price Point)**

"Color Blind" is a structurally sound basement suite that commits a cardinal sin of short-term rental design: **institutional coldness masquerading as cleanliness.** The property demonstrates technical competence (plumbing works, appliances function, bathrooms are sanitary) but fails catastrophically in the emotional and sovereignty dimensions. 

At **$102/night**, guests expect personality, comfort anchors, and a sense of "this person cared about my stay." Instead, they get a 3-star hotel that didn't invest in the basics. The 4.92⭐ rating likely reflects the practicality of the space and owner responsiveness, not the guest experience of *being* in the property.

**Primary Issue:** Monochromatic institutional design (gray/white/cream) with zero warm touches, missing functional anchors, and no "welcome" signals.

**Immediate Fix Potential:** +18 points (to 80/100) with $800-1200 in targeted interventions.
