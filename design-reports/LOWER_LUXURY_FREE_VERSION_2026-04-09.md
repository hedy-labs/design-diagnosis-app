## THREE FIXES TO TRANSFORM YOUR PROPERTY

### 🥇 FIX #1: COMPREHENSIVE LIGHTING TRANSFORMATION
**Impact:** +10–12 points | **Investment:** $400–650 CAD | **Payback:** 1.5 months

Institutional overhead lighting is killing your revenue. Lighting transforms both the space AND how it photographs.

**Key moves:** Brass floor lamps, warm pendant lights, 2700K LED bulbs, dimmer switches.

---

### 🥈 FIX #2: LIFESTYLE STAGING (PLANTS, ART, TEXTILES)
**Impact:** +8–10 points | **Investment:** $250–400 CAD | **Payback:** 2–3 months

Bare rooms feel sterile. Plants + art + throws transform "empty" into "designed" in 48 hours.

**Key moves:** Potted plants, wall art, area rug, throw pillows, textured throws, bedside lamps.

---

### 🥉 FIX #3: BEDROOM RECONFIGURATION + KITCHEN OPTIMIZATION
**Impact:** +6–8 points | **Investment:** $350–550 CAD | **Payback:** 1.5–2.5 months

Current layout has critical dysfunction: settee blocks fridge, bedrooms are cramped, zero entertainment.

**Key moves:** Remove settee, reconfigure bedrooms (choose single-bed hotel-style OR two-bed config), upgrade kitchen nook lighting, optimize secondary bedroom.
