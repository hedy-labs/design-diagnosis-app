## THREE FIXES TO TRANSFORM YOUR PROPERTY

### 🥇 FIX #1: COMPREHENSIVE LIGHTING TRANSFORMATION
**Impact:** +10–12 points | **Investment:** $400–650 CAD | **Payback:** 1.5 months

**Why it matters:** Institutional overhead lighting is killing your revenue. Every guest photo comparison will favor warmer competitors. Lighting transforms both the space AND how it photographs.

**What to buy:**
- 3–4 brass/gold floor lamps ($120–180 ea) — Wayfair.ca or Article
- 2–3 warm brass pendant lights for dining area ($80–150 ea) — Wayfair.ca
- 25+ 2700K LED warm white bulbs ($0.80 ea) — Amazon.ca (roomsbyrachel-20)
- 4–5 dimmer switches ($30 ea) — Home Depot.ca

**Installation:** Electrician 2–3 hours ($150–250) OR DIY if comfortable. Bulbs = 30 min.

**Expected result:** From "depressing, institutional" to "warm, welcoming, luxury retreat." Photos improve 40% immediately.

---

### 🥈 FIX #2: LIFESTYLE STAGING (PLANTS, ART, TEXTILES)
**Impact:** +8–10 points | **Investment:** $250–400 CAD | **Payback:** 2–3 months

**Why it matters:** Bare rooms feel sterile. Plants + art + throws transform "empty" into "designed" in 48 hours. Highest ROI per dollar.

**What to buy:**
- 5–7 potted plants ($12–25 ea) — Local nursery or Amazon.ca
- 3–4 contemporary wall art prints ($40–80 ea) — Wayfair.ca or Etsy
- 1 area rug, 8x10, grey/warm tone ($100–200) — Wayfair.ca
- 5–6 throw pillows ($25–50 ea) — Wayfair.ca or Article
- 2–3 textured throws ($40–80 ea) — Amazon.ca or H&M Home
- 2 bedside lamps ($60–120 ea) — Wayfair.ca or IKEA.ca

**Installation:** Styling 4–6 hours (DIY or hire staging assistant $100–150). Art hanging = 2 hours DIY.

**Expected result:** From "bare, uninviting" to "designed, cared-for, Instagram-worthy." Guest reviews shift +30–40% in tone.

---

### 🥉 FIX #3: BEDROOM RECONFIGURATION + KITCHEN OPTIMIZATION
**Impact:** +6–8 points | **Investment:** $350–550 CAD | **Payback:** 1.5–2.5 months

**Why it matters:** Current layout has critical dysfunction: settee blocks the fridge, bedrooms are cramped, zero entertainment. Smart reconfiguration fixes all three.

**What to do:**
- **Remove the settee** (donate/sell) — Frees kitchen space, improves flow

**Primary Bedroom — Choose One:**
- **Option A: Two-Bed Configuration** — Keep both beds but separate them, add ONE shared nightstand + ONE shared lamp between beds ($100–150). Mount TV on wall for entertainment ($100–150). **Best for:** Families, groups of friends. Note: Still feels cramped; minimal personal space.
- **Option B: Single Bed Hotel-Style Suite** — Remove one bed, install ONE queen/double bed as focal point. Add ONE nightstand + ONE bedside lamp beside bed ($150–200). Add small comfortable chair + side table facing the bed ($150–250). Mount TV on wall. **Best for:** Solo travelers, couples, professionals, romantic getaways. **Best ROI:** Converts cramped space into hotel-quality room. Guests feel dignified + comfortable.

**Secondary Bedroom — Choose One:**
- **Option A: Guest Bedroom (pairs with Primary Option A):** Nightstands + bedside lamps ($150–200). Ideal if primary is two-bed configuration and you want second option for overflow guests.
- **Option B: Den/Lounge (pairs with Primary Option B):** Small sofa, footstool, side table, lamps ($250–400). Creates alternative gathering space; appeals to couples/romantic getaways.

- **Kitchen nook:** Upgrade lighting with pendant or accent lights ($100–150)

**Installation:** Remove settee 1 hour. Furniture delivery 3–5 days. TV install 1–2 hours (electrician or DIY). Total labor: 1–2 days.

**Expected result:** From "cramped, dysfunctional, temporary" to "smart use of space, guest-respectful, modern, intentional."

**Layout note:** This property doesn't have a traditional living room—just a kitchen nook. Living room expansion isn't feasible. These fixes optimize what exists.
