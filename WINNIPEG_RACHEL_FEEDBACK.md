- ✅ New rule: "Desk facing wall in micro-space = -0.5 (constraint-based, not design failure)"

### 3. **Artwork = Placement, Not Just Count** (Pillar 5)
- ❌ Old rule: "Count total pieces ÷ rooms"
- ✅ New rule: "Identify required focal points per space (above bed, above dining table, above sofa). Assess placement quality + strategic relevance."
- **New Framework**: 
  - Focal-point art missing = -0.5 per location

### 4. **Recognize Hidden Strengths** (Pillar 5)

- Don't just look for deficits; note what's well-executed

### 5. **Functional Anchors are Non-Negotiable** (Pillar 1)
- End tables, coffee table, dresser (clothing storage) = CRITICAL for guest comfort
- Not decorative; these are functional essentials
- Missing = -1.0 per anchor type

### 6. **Micro-Space Textile Fixes** (Pillar 3)
- When space is tight, textile solutions (runners, placemats, cushions, throws) become even MORE critical
- These are the "easy wins" for designers in constrained spaces
- Emphasize textile fixes + accent lighting (wall sconce) for dining areas
