## 💡 Top 3 Recommended Fixes

### **#1 — Add Throw Pillows + Coordinating Textiles** ⭐ HIGH ROI
**Cost:** $150–$350 CAD  
**Effort:** 3 hours (sourcing + styling)  
**Impact:** +2–3 points (Color Harmony & Staging) | Guest perception boost ~18%

**Why:** Your two gray sofas are fine and show intentional design—but they need textiles to warm the space and create visual interest. Throw pillows in warm jewel tones (ochre, terracotta, sage, rust) will transform the living room from sterile to inviting.

**Specific Action Items:**
- 4–6 throw pillows in warm earth tones + jewel tones ($80–$150)
- Warm-toned area rug under seating (rust, warm taupe) ($80–$120)
- Coordinated throws/blankets on sofas ($40–$80)

**Shopping Guide:**
- Budget: Wayfair, Article
- Mid-range: West Elm, CB2
- Premium: Schoolhouse Electric, Restoration Hardware

**Timeline:** Week 1–2

---

### **#2 — Expand Plant Strategy** ⭐ MEDIUM ROI
**Cost:** $120–$280 CAD  
**Effort:** 2 hours (purchasing + positioning)  
**Impact:** +1–2 points (Styling & Cohesion) | Guest perception boost ~10%

**Why:** You have small plants in the living room corner, but they're insufficient for styling impact. Expand to 3–5 more plants in larger specimens, grouped in 2–3 zones throughout the home. This adds life, warmth, and sophistication without major investment.

**Specific Action Items:**
- Living room grouping: 2 large floor plants (fiddle leaf fig, monstera) + 2 potted accents ($80–$120)
- Master bedroom: 1 large plant + 1 trailing plant ($40–$60)
- Bathroom: 1 humidity-loving plant (pothos, fern) ($20–$30)
- Use coordinated planters (warm terracotta or natural ceramic) for cohesion ($30–$50)

**Note:** Plants remain a secondary styling element (Decor score stays at 38/100 due to other major gaps like color palette). However, expanded greenery signals care and sophistication.

**Shopping Guide:**
- Budget: Home Depot, local nurseries
- Mid-range: Patch Plants, Bloomscape
- Premium: Local specialty growers, design nurseries

**Timeline:** Week 2–3

---

### **#3 — Add Accent Lighting** ⭐ HIGH ROI
**Cost:** $200–$450 CAD  
**Effort:** 3 hours (installation + styling)  
**Impact:** +1–2 points (Lighting Quality) | 8–12% nightly rate increase possible

**Why:** Strategic accent lighting transforms perceived value and coziness. Your space currently relies on ceiling fixtures + bedside lamps. Add arc lamps, wall sconces, or pendant lights to create layered, warm lighting that feels luxe and guest-ready.
